What to Include

The details that help the job get routed correctly

Customers often speed up the process by sharing the service type, the property location, whether the vehicle is trapped, and whether the door is unsafe to operate.

For repair calls:

Describe the symptom. Examples include broken spring, off-track door, panel damage, grinding noise, opener not responding, or safety sensor issue.

For replacement or installation:

Share the property location, whether insulation matters, and whether opener work should be included in the estimate.
