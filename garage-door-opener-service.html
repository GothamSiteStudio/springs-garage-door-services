Common Issues

Garage door opener problems we fix in Colorado Springs

Most opener calls come down to a handful of repeat issues. Some are quick fixes, others mean it is time to upgrade the unit.

Opener won't respond

Remote works but wall switch doesn't (or vice versa)? Logic board, wiring, or button failure are usually the cause. We diagnose before recommending replacement.

Door reverses on close

Misaligned safety sensors are the most common cause. Realignment, lens cleaning, or sensor replacement usually solves it within minutes.

Loud or grinding opener

Worn drive gear, stripped chain, or motor wear. Older chain drives can often be replaced with a quieter belt drive at a reasonable cost.

Remote and keypad issues

Reprogramming, battery replacement, frequency interference, or full keypad swap. We carry common replacement remotes and can pair them on-site.

Smart opener setup

myQ, HomeLink, Apple HomeKit, Google Home. We set up smartphone access and integrate with your existing smart-home equipment.

Full opener replacement

If your opener is more than 10-15 years old or repeatedly failing, replacement is often more cost-effective than repair. We help you weigh the call honestly.

Drive Types

Belt drive vs chain drive vs wall-mount

Belt drive is the quietest option, ideal for attached garages and bedrooms above. Chain drive is durable and budget-friendly, great for detached or commercial spaces. Wall-mount (jackshaft) openers free up ceiling space and work well for high-lift or low-headroom garages.
