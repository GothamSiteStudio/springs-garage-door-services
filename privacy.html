Privacy Policy

How Springs Garage Door Services handles website and contact information

This policy explains what information may be collected through the website, how it is used to respond to service requests, and how customers can reach the business with privacy questions.

Contact requests Website interactions Customer communication
1. Information collected

What information may be submitted

When a visitor calls, emails, or uses the contact form, Springs Garage Door Services may receive information such as name, phone number, email address, service location, service type, urgency, and details about the garage door issue.

2. How information is used

Why the business collects it

Customer information is used to respond to service requests, confirm service areas, communicate about scheduling, prepare estimates, and follow up on repair, replacement, installation, opener, maintenance, or spring service inquiries.

3. Form and email submissions

Website forms are for service communication

The quote form on this site is intended to help route leads efficiently. Form details should not include sensitive payment information or unrelated private information. A live inbox, CRM, or other workflow may be connected later as the business expands its lead handling.

4. Information sharing

Sharing is limited to operational needs

Springs Garage Door Services does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to operate the site, support communications, or complete requested services, such as through email, hosting, analytics, or scheduling tools.

5. External platforms

Maps, social profiles, and third-party tools

The website may link to Google Maps, review platforms, or social media pages. Those third-party services have their own privacy practices, and Springs Garage Door Services is not responsible for how those platforms collect or use data.

6. Data retention

Information is kept only as needed

Submitted information may be retained for customer service, scheduling, follow-up, and recordkeeping purposes for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the business or meet legal obligations.

7. Your choices

How to ask questions or request updates

If you want to correct contact details, ask how your information is being used, or request that a service inquiry be removed from active follow-up records, contact Springs Garage Door Services directly using the business phone number or email address listed on the site.

8. Policy changes

This page may be updated

This Privacy Policy may be updated as website tools, analytics, lead handling, or service workflows change. The current version on the site is the version that applies.

Privacy Questions

Reach the business directly if you need a clarification.

Customers who want to ask about website submissions, email contact, or service-request communication can use the standard business contact details.
