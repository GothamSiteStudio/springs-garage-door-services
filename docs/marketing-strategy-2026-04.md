**Prepared:** April 18, 2026
**Business:** Springs Garage Door Services, Colorado Springs, CO
**Owner:** Eyal | Phone: 719-308-9951
**Website:** springsgaragedoorservices.com (static HTML, GitHub Pages)

---

## Strategic Diagnosis

Springs Garage Door Services is an owner-operated local garage door company with strong service quality (5.0 Google rating) but near-zero digital visibility. The site recently migrated from Wix to static HTML and is suffering from critical technical SEO issues: a www/non-www canonical conflict, ghost Wix URLs still indexed, and key pages (services.html, contact.html) not in Google's index at all.

With 1,935 impressions but only 2 clicks (0.1% CTR) at an average position of 12.2, the business is invisible in search. The site has no analytics tracking, no email list, no paid campaigns, and only 1 backlink. Despite this, the business has a real differentiator: Eyal answers the phone, shows up, does the work, and communicates directly. This personal touch is rare in the garage door industry and is the foundation of the brand.

**Core challenge:** The business has a great service but no one can find it online. Every strategy recommendation below is designed to generate phone calls and quote requests from local homeowners.

## 4. Media Mix Recommendation

**Monthly budget assumption:** $300-$500/month (owner time + small spend)

| Channel | % of Effort | Monthly Spend | Owner Time/Week | Priority |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| **SEO (Technical + Content)** | 35% | $0 (sweat equity) | 3-4 hours | #1 |
| **Google Business Profile** | 25% | $0 | 2-3 hours | #2 |
| **Reviews & Reputation** | 15% | $0 | 1 hour | #3 |
| **Social Media (FB + Nextdoor)** | 10% | $0-50/month boosting | 1-2 hours | #4 |
| **Directory Listings** | 5% | $0-100/month (if Thumbtack/Angi) | 30 min setup | #5 |
| **Google Local Services Ads** | 10% | $200-300/month (start at 90 days) | 30 min/week | #6 (Phase 2) |

### Why This Mix

- **SEO is #1** because the GSC data shows demand exists (1,935 impressions) but the site has critical technical blocks preventing clicks. Fixing these is free and has the highest ROI.
- **GBP is #2** because for "near me" and local-intent searches, the Local Pack (Maps 3-pack) drives more calls than organic results. Reviews and GBP activity directly influence pack ranking.
- **Reviews are #3** because 5.0 rating with low count loses to 4.8 with 200 reviews. Volume builds trust and improves local pack ranking.
- **Social is #4** because it builds brand awareness and trust but does not directly generate emergency leads. Keep it light.
- **Paid ads are Phase 2** (90 days) because spending money before the site is indexed and converting properly wastes budget. Fix the foundation first.

## 5. Positioning Statement & Brand Voice

### Positioning Statement

> For Colorado Springs homeowners who need garage door repair, installation, or replacement, Springs Garage Door Services is the owner-operated company where Eyal personally answers your call, shows up on time, and does the work himself. Unlike large franchise operations that send a different technician every time, we provide direct communication, honest recommendations, and consistent quality from the same person every visit.

### Brand Differentiators (Rank Order)

1. **Owner-operated:** Eyal answers, Eyal shows up, Eyal does the work
2. **Direct communication:** No call centers, no dispatchers, no runaround
3. **Honest recommendations:** Repair when possible, replace only when necessary
4. **Local knowledge:** Based in Colorado Springs, serves the community daily
5. **7-day availability:** Mon-Fri 7am-8pm, weekends 8am-8pm

### Brand Voice Guidelines

| Attribute | Do | Don't |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| **Tone** | Friendly, direct, confident, helpful | Salesy, pushy, corporate, overly casual |
| **Language** | Plain English, short sentences, active voice | Jargon, filler words, passive voice |
| **Personality** | Like talking to a knowledgeable neighbor | Like reading a corporate brochure |
| **Trust signals** | "I'll take a look and give you an honest recommendation" | "Our team of certified experts will assess your situation" |
| **Urgency** | "Call me directly and I'll get back to you fast" | "LIMITED TIME OFFER! ACT NOW!" |

### Voice Examples

- **Website CTA:** "Call Eyal directly: 719-308-9951"
- **GMB post:** "Replaced a broken torsion spring in Monument today. Homeowner said the door had been stuck since yesterday morning. Back up and running in under an hour."
- **Review response:** "Thanks for the kind words, [Name]. Glad I could get your door working again. Let me know if you ever need anything."
