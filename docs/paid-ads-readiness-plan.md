## 1. Google Local Services Ads (LSA)

### What LSA Is
Google LSA appears above all other search results (above regular Google Ads) with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. You pay per lead (phone call or message), not per click. For garage door services, this is the highest-ROI paid channel.

### Eligibility Requirements

| Requirement | Status | Action Needed |
|-------------|--------|---------------|
| Google Business Profile (verified) | Likely active | Confirm verification is complete |
| Business license (CO) | Unknown | Eyal must confirm he has a valid business license |
| General liability insurance | Unknown | Must provide proof of insurance |
| Background check (owner + employees) | Not done | Google runs this through Pinkerton; takes 2-5 weeks |
| Service area match | Colorado Springs area | Already defined |
| Business category: Garage Door | Must select | Set during LSA signup |

### LSA Setup Steps
1. Go to ads.google.com/local-services-ads
2. Create profile: business name, address, phone, service area
3. Select category: **Garage Door** (includes repair, installation, opener)
4. Upload proof of insurance (general liability, minimum $500K recommended)
5. Upload business license
6. Submit to background check (Eyal + any employees who enter homes)
7. Set weekly budget
8. Wait for Google approval (typically 2-5 weeks for background check)

### Expected LSA Costs (Garage Door, Colorado Springs)

| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|----------|
| Cost per lead | $25 - $50 |
| Lead type | Phone calls + messages |
| Minimum weekly budget | $100 - $150 |
| Monthly spend (testing) | $400 - $600 |
| Expected leads/month at $500 | 10 - 20 leads |
| Close rate (industry avg) | 30 - 50% |
| Revenue per job (repair avg) | $200 - $500 |
| Revenue per job (install avg) | $800 - $2,500 |

**ROI math at $500/month:** 10-20 leads x 40% close rate = 4-8 jobs. Even at $300 avg revenue = $1,200 - $2,400 revenue on $500 spend. **LSA should be the first paid channel launched.**

### LSA Advantages for This Business
- Pay per lead, not per click (no wasted spend on non-converters)
- Google Guaranteed badge builds trust for a newer business
- Appears above regular search ads
- No landing page needed (leads go directly to phone/GBP messaging)
- Simple to manage for an owner-operator

| Business name, address, phone consistent | Yes | Present in schema |

### Ideal: Dedicated Landing Pages (Phase 2)

For better Quality Score and conversion rate, create:

1. **`/garage-door-repair-colorado-springs.html`** (for Repair campaign)
   - Hero: "Garage Door Broken? Same-Day Repair in Colorado Springs"
   - Prominent phone number and click-to-call
   - List of repair services (springs, cables, openers, off-track)
   - Trust: owner photo, reviews, licensed/insured badges
   - Single CTA: Call or fill out emergency form

2. **`/garage-door-installation-colorado-springs.html`** (for Installation campaign)
   - Hero: "New Garage Door Installation - Free Quote"
   - Gallery of door styles / brands
   - Process explanation (consult > measure > install)
   - Trust signals
   - CTA: "Get a Free Installation Quote"

### Quality Score Impact

| Factor | Weight | How Landing Pages Help |
|--------|--------|----------------------|
| Expected CTR | ~39% | Better ad relevance from matched pages |
| Ad relevance | ~22% | Keywords match page content |
| Landing page experience | ~39% | Fast, relevant, mobile-friendly |

A Quality Score of 7+ (vs. 5 default) reduces CPC by ~28%. At small budgets, this means 28% more clicks for the same money.

```html
<script>
  window.dataLayer = window.dataLayer || [];
  function gtag(){dataLayer.push(arguments);}
  gtag('js', new Date());
  gtag('config', 'G-XXXXXXXXXX');
</script>
```

### Phone Click Tracking (add to main.js)

```javascript
// Track phone link clicks as GA4 conversion events
document.querySelectorAll('a[href^="tel:"]').forEach(function(link) {
  link.addEventListener('click', function() {
    gtag('event', 'phone_call_click', {
      event_category: 'contact',
      event_label: this.href,
      value: 1
    });
  });
});
```
